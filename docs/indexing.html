maurizio_berta

I'm a music composer, researcher and software developer exploring the use of machine learning in music and computer assisted composition.

I'm currently living in Stockholm where I'm a PhD Student at the Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) in Stockholm as part of the project "The sound of the invisible: Methods for communicating complex and inaccessible information through sound".
I have a Master's degree in Electronic Music from the Conservatory G.Verdi of Turin and a Bachelor degree in Electronic Music for the Audiovisual from the Civica Scuola di Musica Claudio Abbado of Milan.
I collaborated for several years with the ETS association InLuogo and director Valeria Fornoni.
I had the opportunity to study with A. Agostini, A. Valle, D. Ghisi, D. Sciajno, S. Bassanese. And I followed masterclasses by Alvin Curran, Bjarni Gunnarsson, Mauro Lanza, Yann Orlarey, Michele Tadini, Kees Taazelar, Walter Grimmer.
