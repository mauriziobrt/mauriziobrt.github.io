An **immersive sound walk through the symbolic sites of Scalo di Porta Romana and Piazza Olivetti**.

After an initial experience created in 2023, Inluogo returns to explore the neighborhood, which has undergone profound transformation over the past two years.

Through historical research, interviews with residents, and contributions from experts, the project reflects on the **construction of the future Milano–Cortina 2026 Olympic Village and the changes currently underway**. The sound map, guided by the voice of actress Anahì Traversi, offers a **critical and sensitive perspective on the present and future of the area**, moving between **gentrification**, **memory**, and a **desire for community**.
